<p><p>The micro inverter market can be categorized into single-phase and three-phase systems. Single-phase micro inverters are typically used in residential settings, allowing for easy installation and integration into home solar systems. They are suitable for lower energy demands. In contrast, three-phase micro inverters are designed for commercial and industrial applications, providing higher efficiency and the ability to manage larger energy loads. These systems are often favored for their enhanced reliability and performance in more demanding environments.</p></p>
