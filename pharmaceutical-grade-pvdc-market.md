<p><p>The pharmaceutical grade PVDC market is divided into two main types: PVDC resins and PVDC latex. PVDC resins are solid forms used primarily for packaging applications, offering excellent barrier properties against moisture, gases, and odors. They are ideal for protecting sensitive pharmaceuticals. PVDC latex, on the other hand, is a water-based dispersion suitable for coatings and films, providing flexibility and ease of application. Both types are essential for enhancing the stability and shelf life of pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
