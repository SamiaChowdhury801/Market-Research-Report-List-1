<p><p>The Adult Clinical Nutrition market encompasses various types of nutritional support: Oral Nutrition, Enteral Nutrition, and Parenteral Nutrition. Oral Nutrition involves the use of nutritional supplements to enhance dietary intake for those who can eat but require additional nutrients. Enteral Nutrition is delivered directly to the gastrointestinal tract via tubes, ideal for patients unable to consume food orally. Parenteral Nutrition provides essential nutrients intravenously, suitable for individuals with digestive disorders or severe malnutrition who cannot ingest food orally or enterally.</p></p>
