<p><p>Shelf life testing involves evaluating how long a product remains effective and safe for consumption. The real-time market refers to testing conducted under normal storage conditions, where products are periodically evaluated over their expected lifespan. In contrast, the accelerated market involves simulating conditions—such as increased temperature and humidity—to hasten the aging process, enabling quicker assessments of stability and potency. Both methods are essential for determining the appropriate expiration dates and ensuring product quality.</p></p>
