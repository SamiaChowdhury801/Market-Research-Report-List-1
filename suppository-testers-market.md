<p><strong>The Suppository Testers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Disintegration Tester</li><li>Hardness Tester</li><li>Melting Point Tester</li><li>Penetration Tester</li><li>Dissolution Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The suppository testers market encompasses several key types that assess the quality and performance of suppositories. Disintegration testers evaluate how quickly a suppository breaks down in a fluid environment. Hardness testers measure the structural integrity and strength of the suppository. Melting point testers determine the temperature at which the suppository transitions to liquid, ensuring proper release. Penetration testers assess the ease of insertion, while dissolution testers gauge how effectively the active ingredients are released, ensuring therapeutic efficacy.</p></p>
