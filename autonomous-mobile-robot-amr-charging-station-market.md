<p><p>The Autonomous Mobile Robot (AMR) charging station market is categorized into two main types: wireless AMR charging stations and physical contact-based charging stations. Wireless AMR charging stations utilize electromagnetic fields for power transmission, allowing robots to recharge without direct contact, enhancing convenience and reducing wear. In contrast, physical contact-based charging stations require robots to dock with connectors to establish a direct electrical link for charging. Both types offer distinct advantages, catering to varying operational needs and environments.</p></p>
