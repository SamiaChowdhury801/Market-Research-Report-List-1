<p><strong>The TMR Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Output</li><li>Analog Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TMR (Tunnel Magnetoresistance) sensors are classified into two primary market types based on their output: digital and analog. Digital output TMR sensors provide discrete signals indicating on/off states, making them suitable for applications requiring precise switching, such as in data storage devices. Analog output TMR sensors, on the other hand, deliver continuous voltage or current signals that correlate with the magnetic field strength, ideal for applications needing real-time monitoring or measurement, like automotive and industrial systems.</p></p>
