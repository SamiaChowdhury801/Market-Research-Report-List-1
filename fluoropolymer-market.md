<p><p>The fluoropolymer market consists of two main types: solid and solution. Solid fluoropolymers are available in forms such as films, sheets, and coatings, widely used in applications requiring high chemical resistance and thermal stability. Solution fluoropolymers, on the other hand, are dispersed in solvents for applications like coatings and adhesives. Solid types are favored for their durability and robustness, while solution forms offer versatility in application methods, making them suitable for various industrial processes and consumer products.</p></p>
