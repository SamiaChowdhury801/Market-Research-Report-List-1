<p><p>Investment accounting software is categorized into two main market types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises solutions are installed locally on a company's servers, offering complete control over data and compliance but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. Both types serve diverse organizational needs, catering to different preferences in data security and cost management.</p></p>
