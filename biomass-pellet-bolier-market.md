<p><p>The biomass pellet boiler market comprises three main types: fully-automatic, semi-automatic, and manual pellet boilers. Fully-automatic pellet boilers offer the highest convenience, featuring advanced controls and minimal user intervention for fuel feeding and operation. Semi-automatic pellet boilers require some manual input for ignition and maintenance but automate fuel feeding. Manual pellet boilers necessitate significant user involvement in all operational aspects, including fuel loading and maintenance. Each type caters to different consumer preferences regarding convenience, control, and operational effort.</p></p>
