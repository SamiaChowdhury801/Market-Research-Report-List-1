<p><p>Multicast Switches (MCS) facilitate simultaneous data transmission to multiple destinations, essential in telecommunications and data centers. The market is primarily divided into PLC (Planar Lightwave Circuit) based and MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) based switches. PLC-based switches offer compact, high-performance solutions with low loss and scalability, making them suitable for large networks. In contrast, MEMS-based switches provide high reliability and flexibility, ideal for dynamic and adaptable networking environments. Both technologies cater to diverse applications in optical networks and data management.</p></p>
