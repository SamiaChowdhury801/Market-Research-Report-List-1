<p><p>Absorbable sutures are surgical threads designed to dissolve over time within the body, eliminating the need for removal after healing. These sutures are commonly made from materials such as polyglycolic acid, polyglactin, and catgut, and they are widely used in various surgical procedures including orthopedic, gynecological, and general surgeries. The benefits of absorbable sutures include reduced patient discomfort, decreased risk of infection, and minimized scarring.</p><p>The absorbable sutures market is poised for substantial growth, driven by an increasing demand for minimally invasive surgical procedures and advancements in medical technology. Rising awareness of the benefits of absorbable sutures, coupled with a growing geriatric population prone to surgical interventions, further propels market expansion. Additionally, the development of innovative suture materials that offer higher tensile strength and faster absorption times is gaining traction.</p><p>The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 3.50% during the forecast period.
