<p><strong>The Read Only MemoryROM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mask ROM</li><li>PROM</li><li>EPROM</li><li>EEPROM</li><li>Flash Memory</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Read-Only Memory (ROM) market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Mask ROM** is pre-programmed during manufacturing, offering high speed and low cost but inflexibility.</p><p>2. **PROM** can be programmed once after manufacture, suitable for specific applications. </p><p>3. **EPROM** allows erasure and reprogramming using UV light, facilitating updates. </p><p>4. **EEPROM** enables electrical erasure and reprogramming, allowing selective data updates. </p><p>5. **Flash Memory** is a type of EEPROM that allows rapid erasure and programming of large blocks, enhancing performance in consumer electronics.</p></p>
