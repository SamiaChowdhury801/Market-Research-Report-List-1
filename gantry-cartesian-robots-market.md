<p><p>Gantry (Cartesian) robots are classified by their axis configurations, impacting their capabilities and applications. </p><p>1-Axis robots operate along a single linear path, suitable for straightforward tasks like pick-and-place. </p><p>2-Axis robots add another linear motion, enabling more complex movements like routing or simple assembly. </p><p>3-Axis robots can move along three dimensions, allowing for precise positioning in applications like machining. </p><p>4-Axis robots introduce rotational movement, enhancing flexibility for tasks such as welding and intricate assembly processes.</p></p>
