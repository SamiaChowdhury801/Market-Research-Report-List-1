<p><p>A nasal feeding tube is a medical device used for the delivery of nutrition directly into the stomach for patients who are unable to consume food orally. Typically made of flexible material, these tubes are inserted through the nostril and extend to the stomach or small intestine. They are crucial for patients with swallowing difficulties, neurological disorders, or those recovering from surgery.</p><p>The nasal feeding tube market is experiencing significant growth, fueled by rising incidences of chronic diseases such as cancer and neurological disorders that necessitate enteral feeding. Increased awareness about nutritional support and advancements in tube designs for enhanced patient comfort further drive market expansion. The growing geriatric population, often facing difficulty in eating independently, contributes to the rising demand as well.</p><p>Additionally, the COVID-19 pandemic highlighted the importance of alternative feeding methods for critically ill patients, further propelling market growth.
