<p><strong>The Interline Transfer CCD Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-Frame CCD Image Sensors</li><li>Frame-Transfer CCD Image Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Interline Transfer CCD Image Sensors are classified into two primary types: Full-Frame CCD Image Sensors and Frame-Transfer CCD Image Sensors. Full-Frame sensors capture images in a single exposure, offering high sensitivity and image quality but requiring longer readout times. In contrast, Frame-Transfer sensors feature a storage area that allows for faster readout by transferring the captured image to a separate region, enabling quicker successive captures. Both types serve various applications in photography, scientific imaging, and industrial use.</p></p>
