<p><p>The Long-Range Lidars market features two primary types: Single Line Lidars and Multi-Line Lidars. Single Line Lidars utilize a single laser beam to measure distances, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, typically suited for applications like basic obstacle detection. Conversely, Multi-Line Lidars deploy multiple laser beams, enabling simultaneous distance measurements across various angles, enhancing spatial awareness and data accuracy. This makes them ideal for complex environments such as autonomous vehicles and advanced mapping, where detailed 3D representations are crucial.</p></p>
