<p><ul><li>Densified silica fume</li><li>Semi densified silica fume</li><li>Undensified silica fume</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silica fume market is categorized into three main types: densified silica fume, semi-densified silica fume, and undensified silica fume. Densified silica fume is compacted to reduce dust, enhancing handling and application in concrete. Semi-densified silica fume has partial densification, balancing dust control with cost-effectiveness. Undensified silica fume, in its raw form, is often more economical but can create handling challenges due to dust. Each type serves specific applications in construction, enhancing concrete strength and durability.</p></p>
