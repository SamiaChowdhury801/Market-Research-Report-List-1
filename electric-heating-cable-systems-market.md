<p><p>Electric heating cable systems consist of various market types tailored for specific applications. Self-regulating electric heating cables automatically adjust their heat output based on ambient temperature changes, making them energy-efficient. Constant wattage cables deliver consistent heat along their length, suitable for maintaining specific temperatures. Mineral insulated cables provide high-temperature resistance and durability, often used in industrial settings. Skin-effect cables utilize electromagnetic effects to distribute heat, primarily in specialized applications like pipelines and tanks, ensuring reliable thermal management.</p></p>
