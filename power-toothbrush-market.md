<p><p>The power toothbrush market is primarily categorized into two types: vibration and rotation-oscillation. Vibration toothbrushes utilize high-frequency movements to enhance cleaning efficiency, effectively disrupting plaque and debris. In contrast, rotation-oscillation toothbrushes feature a rotating bristle head that sweeps back and forth, effectively targeting plaque in hard-to-reach areas. Both technologies promote better oral hygiene compared to manual brushing, but they differ in their mechanisms and performance benefits, catering to varied consumer preferences and needs for dental care.</p></p>
