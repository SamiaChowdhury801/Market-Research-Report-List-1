<p><p>The rear view mirror replacement market is evolving with technological advancements, primarily focusing on CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras are known for their superior image quality and low-light performance, making them suitable for high-end vehicles. In contrast, CMOS cameras offer lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and are more cost-effective, appealing to mass-market vehicles. Both technologies enhance safety and driving experience by providing clearer rear visibility and advanced features like object detection and lane guidance.</p></p>
