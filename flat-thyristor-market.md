<p><p>The Flat Thyristor market comprises three primary types: Diode Thyristor, Triode Thyristor, and Quadrupole Thyristor. Diode Thyristors are used for controlled rectification, allowing specific current flow. Triode Thyristors feature an additional gate terminal for enhanced control over switching functions, making them ideal for applications like motor control and power regulation. Quadrupole Thyristors incorporate four layers and are often utilized in high-power applications due to their robustness and efficiency. Each type serves distinct purposes in power electronics and control systems.</p></p>
