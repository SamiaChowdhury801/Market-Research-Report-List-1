<p><p>The Ethylene Vinyl Infusion Bag market comprises two primary types: single chamber and multi-chamber bags. Single chamber bags feature a single compartment for mixing and storing medical solutions or pharmaceuticals, ensuring straightforward usage. In contrast, multi-chamber bags contain separate compartments that allow for the storage of multiple components, which can be mixed just before administration. This versatility enhances the preservation of sensitive components and allows for tailored dosing, making both types essential for efficient drug delivery and storage in healthcare settings.</p></p>
