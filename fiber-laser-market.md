<p><strong>The Fiber Laser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber laser market is primarily divided into two types: Continuous Wave (CW) fiber lasers and Pulsed fiber lasers. CW fiber lasers emit a constant beam of light, making them ideal for applications requiring steady power such as cutting and welding metals. In contrast, pulsed fiber lasers deliver energy in short bursts, enabling precise material processing and high-speed marking applications. Both types serve various industries, including manufacturing, telecommunications, and medical, each tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
