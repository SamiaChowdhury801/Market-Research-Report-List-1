<p><p>The helideck lighting market encompasses various types essential for safe helicopter operations. Circle-H lights denote the helideck area, providing clear identification. Floodlights illuminate the helideck surface, enhancing visibility during operations. Perimeter lights outline the helideck's edges, ensuring safety awareness. Status lights indicate operational conditions, such as whether the helideck is in use. Windsocks inform pilots of wind direction and speed, while obstruction lights warn of nearby structures, collectively ensuring safe air navigation and landing on helidecks.</p></p>
