<p><p>The hygienic liquid pumps market primarily features two types: single-stage pumps and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed for straightforward applications, efficiently handling low-viscosity fluids in food, beverage, and pharmaceutical industries. They provide reliable performance with simple construction. In contrast, multi-stage pumps are utilized for applications requiring high pressure and flow rates, accommodating more complex processing needs. These pumps are ideal for transferring fluids over long distances or elevating them to high elevations, ensuring consistent hygiene standards throughout.</p></p>
