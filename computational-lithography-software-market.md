<p><p>The Computational Lithography Software market encompasses various techniques that enhance photolithographic processes in semiconductor manufacturing. Optical Proximity Correction (OPC) adjusts mask patterns to counteract optical distortions. Source Mask Optimization (SMO) refines light source and mask design for improved pattern fidelity. Model-based Process Tuning (MPT) integrates modeling to optimize fabrication steps. Iterative Learning Technique (ILT) iteratively refines masks for complex geometries, ensuring precision. Together, these approaches drive advancements in chip performance and miniaturization in the evolving semiconductor industry.</p></p>
