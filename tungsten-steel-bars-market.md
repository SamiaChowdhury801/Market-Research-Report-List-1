Hollow tungsten steel bars, on the other hand, feature a cylindrical hollow core, reducing weight while maintaining structural integrity, often used in applications requiring lightweight materials without compromising strength. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, including aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
