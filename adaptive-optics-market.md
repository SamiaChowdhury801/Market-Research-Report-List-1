<p><strong>The Adaptive Optics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wavefront Sensor</li><li>Wavefront Modulator</li><li>Control System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adaptive optics market consists of three primary types: wavefront sensors, wavefront modulators, and control systems. Wavefront sensors detect distortions in light caused by atmospheric turbulence or optical imperfections, enabling real-time assessment. Wavefront modulators adjust the incoming wavefront using devices like deformable mirrors or liquid crystal panels, correcting distortions. Control systems process the data from wavefront sensors to coordinate the modulators, ensuring optimal performance. Together, these components improve imaging quality in applications such as astronomy, telecommunications, and biomedical fields.</p></p>
