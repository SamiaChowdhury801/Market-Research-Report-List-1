<p><p>The SUV and Pickup Wheel Speed Sensor market primarily features two main types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize a semiconductor to detect changes in magnetic fields, providing precise wheel speed data for stability and traction control systems. Meanwhile, Magnetic Electric sensors operate based on magnetic induction, generating an electrical signal proportional to wheel rotation. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle safety and performance by ensuring accurate speed readings for advanced driver-assistance systems.</p></p>
