<p><p>The biological polymer film market consists of various types, each serving specific applications. PLA films are derived from corn starch and are biodegradable, suitable for packaging. PHB and PHA films are produced from microbial fermentation, offering compostability and versatility. PVA films are water-soluble, used in packaging and agricultural applications. Polyamide films provide mechanical strength and barrier properties. Mulch films enhance soil health in agriculture, while PBAT films blend flexibility with biodegradability, making them ideal for various eco-friendly applications.</p></p>
