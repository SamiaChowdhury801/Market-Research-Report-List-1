<p><p>The fans less than 225mm market consists of axial and centrifugal fans, each serving specific applications. Axial fans utilize a propeller-like design to move air parallel to the fan axis, making them ideal for cooling and ventilation in confined spaces. In contrast, centrifugal fans employ a rotary design to draw air in and expel it at right angles, offering higher pressure capabilities, suitable for industrial and HVAC systems. Both types are crucial for efficient airflow management across various sectors.</p></p>
