<p><ul><li>Emulsion PVDC Latex</li><li>Solution PVDC Latex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVDC latex market comprises two main types: emulsion and solution PVDC latex. Emulsion PVDC latex involves finely dispersed polymer particles in water, offering excellent adhesion and barrier properties, making it ideal for applications like packaging and coating. Solution PVDC latex, on the other hand, is a concentrated version dissolved in organic solvents, providing improved stability and flexibility, commonly used in textiles and adhesives. Both types serve unique roles in various industries, enhancing product performance and durability.</p></p>
