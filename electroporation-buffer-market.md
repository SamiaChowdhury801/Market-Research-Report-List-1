<p><p>Electroporation buffer is a specialized solution used in the electroporation process, which facilitates the introduction of foreign genetic material into cells by creating temporary pores in the cell membrane using an electric field. It typically contains salts, sugars, and other stabilizing agents to protect cells during the electroporation process.</p><p>The Electroporation Buffer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.3% during the forecast period.
