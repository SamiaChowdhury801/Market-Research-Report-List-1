<p><p>The carbon nanotubes powder market for lithium batteries primarily comprises single-walled nanotubes (SWNTs) and multi-walled nanotubes (MWNTs). SWNTs feature a single cylindrical structure that provides exceptional electrical conductivity and mechanical strength, making them ideal for enhancing battery performance. In contrast, MWNTs consist of multiple concentric layers, offering improved structural stability and higher surface area, which can enhance charge storage capacity. Both types are integral in developing advanced lithium batteries with higher efficiency, longevity, and energy density.</p></p>
