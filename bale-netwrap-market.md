<p><ul><li>Knitted Net Wrap</li><li>Extruded Net Wrap</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bale Netwrap market is primarily divided into two types: Knitted Net Wrap and Extruded Net Wrap. Knitted Net Wrap is made from interlocking fibers, offering excellent stretchability and flexibility, making it ideal for securing bales without tight binding. On the other hand, Extruded Net Wrap is produced through a manufacturing process that forms a continuous sheet, providing robust strength and durability. Both types are essential in agriculture for optimizing bale storage and transportation, enhancing fodder protection.</p></p>
