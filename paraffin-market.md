<p><p>The paraffin market is segmented into three main types: fully refined, semi-refined, and crude paraffin. Fully refined paraffin is extensively processed to remove impurities, making it suitable for use in cosmetics, food packaging, and candles. Semi-refined paraffin retains some impurities, making it ideal for applications like lubricants and industrial products. Crude paraffin, extracted directly from source materials, is primarily used in industrial applications and serves as a raw material for further processing. Each type meets distinct industry needs.</p></p>
