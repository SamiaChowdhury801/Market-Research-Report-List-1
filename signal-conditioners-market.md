<p><p>The signal conditioners market primarily consists of two types: analog and digital signal conditioners. Analog signal conditioners process continuous signals, enhancing their quality through amplification, filtering, or linearization to ensure accurate data transmission in industrial applications. In contrast, digital signal conditioners convert analog signals into digital formats, allowing for advanced processing, analysis, and integration with digital systems. Both types are vital in optimizing signal quality for sensors and other devices, ensuring reliable performance across various industries.</p></p>
