<p><p>Orthopedic surgery navigation systems enhance precision in surgical procedures. Optical surgical navigation systems use cameras and tracking devices to create a visual map of the surgical site, allowing for real-time guidance. Electromagnetic surgical navigation systems rely on electromagnetic fields to track instruments and their position relative to the patient's anatomy, providing accurate spatial information. Other market types may include hybrid systems that combine various technologies, advanced imaging solutions, or software enhancements that improve navigation accuracy and overall surgical outcomes.</p></p>
