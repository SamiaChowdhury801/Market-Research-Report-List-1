<p><p>The all-electric satellites market is categorized into three main types based on their orbits: Low Earth Orbit (LEO), Medium Earth Orbit (MEO), and Geosynchronous Orbit (GEO). LEO satellites operate at altitudes of 200 to 2,000 kilometers, providing low-latency communication and Earth observation. MEO satellites, situated between 2,000 to 35,786 kilometers, are often used for navigation and communication services. GEO satellites orbit at around 35,786 kilometers, enabling continuous coverage over specific regions, ideal for broadcasting and weather monitoring.</p></p>
