<p><p>The Synthetic Rubber Hot Melt Adhesive market can be divided into two main types: High-Temperature Vulcanization (HTV) and Room-Temperature Vulcanization (RTV). HTV adhesives are designed for applications requiring higher thermal resistance and are typically used in industries like automotive and aerospace. In contrast, RTV adhesives cure at room temperature, offering ease of application and versatility for general-purpose bonding in home improvement, packaging, and consumer goods. Both types cater to varying temperature and environmental demands, enhancing their market applicability.</p></p>
