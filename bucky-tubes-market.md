<p><ul><li>Singlewall Buckytubes</li><li>Multiwall Buckytubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bucky tubes, or carbon nanotubes, are categorized into two main market types: single-wall and multi-wall. Single-wall buckytubes consist of a single graphene layer rolled into a cylindrical shape, offering superior electrical and thermal conductivity, primarily used in electronics and nanotechnology. Multi-wall buckytubes contain multiple layers of graphene, providing enhanced structural strength and stability, making them ideal for applications in composites and materials science. Both types are integral to advanced technologies, including nanomedicine, energy storage, and sensors.</p></p>
