<p><p>The Acoustic Membrane market primarily comprises two types: Polyurethane (PU) and Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE). PU membranes are known for their flexibility, durability, and ease of installation, making them ideal for various acoustic applications, including soundproofing and noise cancellation. In contrast, PTFE membranes offer superior chemical resistance and thermal stability, typically used in environments where exposure to harsh conditions is a concern. Both materials play crucial roles in enhancing sound management in commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
