<p><p>The SARS-CoV-2 Antigen Test Kit market comprises several types, primarily including the Colloidal Gold Method, ELISA, and Fluorescence Immunochromatography Method. The Colloidal Gold Method utilizes gold nanoparticles to visually indicate the presence of the virus, making it user-friendly and rapid. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) is a more sophisticated approach that quantifies antigen levels through enzyme reactions, offering enhanced sensitivity. Fluorescence Immunochromatography combines immunoassay techniques with fluorescence detection, providing high accuracy and specificity for virus detection. Each method serves distinct needs in rapid COVID-19 diagnostics.</p></p>
