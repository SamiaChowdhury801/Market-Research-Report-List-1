<p><strong>The Fan Coil Unit (FCU) Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two Pipe FCU Valve</li><li>Four Pipe FCU Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fan Coil Unit (FCU) Valve market primarily consists of two types: Two Pipe FCU Valves and Four Pipe FCU Valves. </p><p>Two Pipe FCU Valves allow for either heating or cooling, using a single set of supply and return pipes, making them cost-effective and simpler to install. In contrast, Four Pipe FCU Valves enable simultaneous heating and cooling as they feature separate supply and return pipes for hot and chilled water, providing greater flexibility in temperature control and enhancing overall comfort in HVAC systems.</p></p>
