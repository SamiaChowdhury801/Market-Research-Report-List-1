<p><ul><li>Tapered</li><li>Splined</li><li>Flanged</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft propeller shaft market comprises three main types: tapered, splined, and flanged. Tapered shafts feature a gradual reduction in diameter, allowing for a secure fit and precise alignment in various aircraft types. Splined shafts employ grooves to enhance torque transfer and provide better connection flexibility. Flanged shafts utilize flat surface connections for easy assembly and disassembly, making them suitable for different engine designs. Each type serves specific applications, influencing the overall efficiency and performance of aircraft propulsion systems.</p></p>
