<p><p>The fiberglass rigid board insulation market includes two primary types: semi-rigid boards and rigid boards. Semi-rigid boards offer flexibility while maintaining structural integrity, making them suitable for various applications, such as cavity insulation and other backing layers. Rigid boards, on the other hand, provide a solid, high-performance insulation solution, ideal for exterior walls, roofs, and foundation applications. Both types contribute to energy efficiency and thermal performance in residential and commercial buildings, enhancing overall comfort and lowering energy costs.</p></p>
