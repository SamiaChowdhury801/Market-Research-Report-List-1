<p><p>The Schlieren Imaging System market primarily includes two types: One Mirror Schlieren Imaging and Two Mirror Schlieren Imaging. The One Mirror Schlieren technique uses a single curved mirror to focus light and visualize small density variations in transparent media, making it suitable for simpler applications. In contrast, Two Mirror Schlieren Imaging employs two mirrors to enhance image quality and versatility, allowing for more complex setups and improved visualization of subtle changes in density, thereby catering to advanced scientific research and industrial applications.</p></p>
