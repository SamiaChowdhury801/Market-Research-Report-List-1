<p><p>Scaffolding markets consist of several types, each serving specific construction needs. Frame scaffolding features pre-assembled frames for quick setup, ideal for residential projects. Fastener scaffolds use pins and clips for versatile configurations, suited for various construction environments. Bowl-buckle scaffolding employs a unique bowl and pin connection for fast assembly and stability, making it popular in heavy-duty applications. Other scaffolding types include mobile scaffolding and suspended systems, catering to specific tasks and ensuring worker safety and efficiency on-site.</p></p>
