<p><p>The lung function testers market encompasses various devices used to assess respiratory health. Spirometers measure airflow and lung capacity, while breath CO monitors evaluate carbon monoxide levels in exhaled air, indicating smoking or exposure to pollutants. Oscillometers assess airway resistance through oscillatory pressure waves. Ergospirometers combine exercise testing with metabolic analysis to gauge lung performance during physical activity. Dose-controlled drug nebulizers deliver medication precisely to the lungs. Other devices in this market include peak flow meters and body plethysmographs, enhancing respiratory assessment capabilities.</p></p>
