<p><p>The bitumen market consists of three main types: Natural Bitumen, Petroleum Bitumen, and Coal-Tar Pitch. Natural Bitumen is sourced from natural deposits and is often used in road construction and roofing. Petroleum Bitumen, derived from refining crude oil, is widely utilized in asphalt production for pavements and waterproofing. Coal-Tar Pitch, obtained from coal carbonization, is primarily used in the manufacturing of electrodes and roofing materials. Each type serves distinct industrial applications, shaping the overall bitumen market landscape.</p></p>
