<p><ul><li>Tapered Rings</li><li>Crush Rings</li><li>Steel Rings and Steel Mesh Rings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The exhaust flange gasket market comprises various types, including tapered rings, crush rings, steel rings, and steel mesh rings. Tapered rings provide a tight seal by utilizing a conical design that adapts to flange surfaces. Crush rings compress under pressure to create a leak-proof seal, ideal for high-temperature applications. Steel rings offer durability and resistance to deformation, while steel mesh rings enhance flexibility and strength, accommodating thermal expansion. Each type serves specific requirements in exhaust systems for optimal performance.</p></p>
