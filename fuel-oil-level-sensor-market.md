<p><ul><li>Capacitive Level Sensor</li><li>Optical Level Sensor</li><li>Ultrasonic Level Sensor</li><li>Resistive Level Sensor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel oil level sensor market comprises various types, each utilizing different technologies. Capacitive level sensors measure changes in capacitance to detect fuel levels, while optical level sensors use light refraction or reflection. Ultrasonic level sensors emit sound waves to gauge distance and determine levels, while resistive level sensors rely on resistance changes as fuel level fluctuates. Other sensor types may include radar and gyroscopic sensors, catering to specific applications and providing varied accuracy, reliability, and installation flexibility.</p></p>
