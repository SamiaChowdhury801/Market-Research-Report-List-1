<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Plaque Modification Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thrombectomy Devices</li><li>Atherectomy Devices</li><li>CTO Devices</li><li>EPD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plaque modification devices are critical in treating atherosclerosis-related blockages. Thrombectomy devices remove thrombus from blood vessels, restoring circulation. Atherectomy devices excise or modify plaque directly, allowing for improved vessel patency. Chronic Total Occlusion (CTO) devices facilitate the crossing of completely blocked arteries, enabling further intervention. Embolic Protection Devices (EPDs) capture debris during procedures, preventing downstream complications. Together, these devices enhance the effectiveness of vascular interventions and improve patient outcomes in managing cardiovascular diseases.</p></p>
