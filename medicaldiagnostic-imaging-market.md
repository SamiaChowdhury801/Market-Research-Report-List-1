<p><p>The medical/diagnostic imaging market comprises various modalities that aid in disease detection and patient assessment. X-ray imaging provides quick visualization of bones and soft tissues. Computed Tomography (CT) offers detailed cross-sectional images for comprehensive analysis. Ultrasound equipment utilizes sound waves for real-time imaging, particularly in obstetrics and cardiology. MRI equipment generates detailed images of soft tissues using magnetic fields. Nuclear imaging involves radioactive substances to observe physiological functions, aiding in accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
