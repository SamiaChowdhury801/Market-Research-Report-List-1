<p><strong>The Chromatography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Chromatography (LC)</li><li>Gas Chromatography (GC)</li><li>Supercritical Fluid Chromatography (SFC)</li><li>Thin Layer Chromatography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chromatography market encompasses various techniques, including Liquid Chromatography (LC), which separates compounds in liquid samples, and Gas Chromatography (GC), ideal for volatile substances in gas phase. Supercritical Fluid Chromatography (SFC) utilizes supercritical fluids for high-efficiency separation. Lastly, Thin Layer Chromatography (TLC) involves a stationary phase on a plate, allowing for simple and effective separation of mixtures. Each type offers unique advantages for analytical applications across pharmaceuticals, environmental testing, and food processing sectors.</p></p>
