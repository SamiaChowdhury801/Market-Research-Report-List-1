<p><strong>The Soil Water Moisture Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soil Water Potential</li><li>Volumetric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Soil water moisture sensors are categorized into two primary market types: soil water potential sensors and volumetric water content sensors. Soil water potential sensors measure the energy status of water in the soil, indicating the plant's ability to access moisture. In contrast, volumetric water content sensors quantify the actual volume of water present within a given soil volume. Both types help optimize irrigation, improve agricultural productivity, and manage water resources efficiently by providing real-time data on soil moisture conditions.</p></p>
