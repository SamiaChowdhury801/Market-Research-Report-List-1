<p><strong>The DECT- Phones in B2C Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SIP DECT</li><li>IP DECT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DECT phones in the B2C market are categorized into SIP DECT and IP DECT, each serving different consumer needs. SIP DECT integrates Session Initiation Protocol for seamless VoIP communication, enabling users to make calls over the Internet while retaining advanced features like call forwarding and conferencing. In contrast, IP DECT operates over an IP network, allowing for better mobility and extended range without compromising sound quality. Both types enhance user convenience, offering reliable wireless communication solutions in various home settings.</p></p>
