<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Solventless</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lamination adhesive market is categorized into three main types: solvent-based, solventless, and water-based adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives contain solvents, providing strong bonding but may emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Solventless adhesives, which do not require solvents, are more environmentally friendly and often used in high-performance applications. Water-based adhesives utilize water as a solvent, offering lower VOC emissions and easier cleanup, making them a popular choice for various packaging and laminating needs. Each type has advantages suited to specific applications.</p></p>
