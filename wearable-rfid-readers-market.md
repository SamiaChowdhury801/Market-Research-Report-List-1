<p><p>The wearable RFID readers market is categorized into several types based on the frequency of operation. LF RFID systems operate at low frequencies (125-134 kHz), suitable for applications like animal tracking. HF RFID systems function at high frequencies (13.56 MHz), commonly used in access control and payment systems. UHF RFID systems, which operate in the range of 300 MHz to 3 GHz, are ideal for supply chain management and inventory tracking. Other markets may include specialized applications like NFC technologies and active RFID systems.</p></p>
