<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Mono-Crystal Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace</li><li>Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mono-crystal furnace market includes two primary types: the Czochralski (CZ) method and the Floating Zone (FZ) method. The CZ method involves crystallizing a seed crystal by pulling it upwards from molten material, often used for silicon and germanium production. In contrast, the FZ method utilizes a localized heat zone to melt and crystallize the material as it is drawn upward, providing high-purity crystals. Both methods are essential in producing semiconductor materials for various high-tech applications, impacting electronics and photovoltaics.</p></p>
