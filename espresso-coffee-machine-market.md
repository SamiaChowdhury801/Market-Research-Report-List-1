<p><p>The espresso coffee machine market consists of three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require baristas to control every step, offering a hands-on experience and greater skill expression. Semi-automatic machines automate certain processes, like water delivery, while still allowing user control over grind and timing, appealing to both enthusiasts and casual users. Fully automatic machines handle everything, from grinding to brewing, prioritizing convenience and consistency, making them ideal for users wanting a quick, hassle-free espresso experience.</p></p>
