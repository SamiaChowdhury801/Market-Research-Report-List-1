<p><p>The Softgel Drying Tunnel market primarily consists of two heating types: electric heating and steam heating. Electric heating utilizes resistance elements to generate heat, providing precise temperature control and quick drying times, making it cost-effective for smaller production scales. In contrast, steam heating employs steam to transfer heat, allowing for uniform drying across larger batches. Each method has its advantages, with electric heating favored for efficiency and steam heating preferred for its consistent performance in high-capacity operations.</p></p>
