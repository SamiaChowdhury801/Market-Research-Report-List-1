<p><p>The Fire Retardant PC-ABS market is categorized primarily into two types: Injection Molding and Extrusion Molding. Injection Molding involves injecting molten PC-ABS into molds to create detailed shapes and components, widely used in automotive and electronics due to its precision. Extrusion Molding, on the other hand, forces melted material through a shaped die to produce continuous profiles, ideal for applications like tubing and sheets. Both methods capitalize on PC-ABS's excellent fire resistance and durability, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
