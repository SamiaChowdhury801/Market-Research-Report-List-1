<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle EGR Systems market is divided into two primary types: Electric EGR Valves and Pneumatic EGR Valves. Electric EGR Valves utilize electric motors for precise control of exhaust gas recirculation, enhancing efficiency and emissions performance in modern engines. In contrast, Pneumatic EGR Valves use air pressure for operation, often offering a simpler design suitable for certain applications. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing combustion and reducing nitrogen oxide emissions, catering to the evolving regulatory landscape in the commercial vehicle sector.</p></p>
