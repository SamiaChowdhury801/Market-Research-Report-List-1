<p><p>The office dividers market is primarily divided into two types: desk-mounted and floor-standing office dividers. Desk-mounted office dividers are designed to attach directly to workstations, providing employees with personal space while maintaining an open office environment. They are ideal for collaborative spaces, offering acoustic and visual privacy. Conversely, floor-standing office dividers are independent structures that can define larger areas within an office, allowing for flexible room configurations and improved sound absorption, catering to diverse workspace needs.</p></p>
