<p><p>The lithium-ion battery additives market is categorized into organic and inorganic types. Organic additives, typically derived from carbon-based compounds, enhance battery performance by improving conductivity, thermal stability, and cycle life. In contrast, inorganic additives, often composed of minerals or metal oxides, contribute by enhancing safety, reducing degradation, and facilitating ion transport. Both types serve to optimize the efficiency, safety, and longevity of lithium-ion batteries, which are essential for various applications, including electric vehicles and renewable energy storage systems.</p></p>
