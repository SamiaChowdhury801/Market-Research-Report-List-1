<p><p>The cytogenetic systems market encompasses techniques used to analyze chromosomes for genetic anomalies. Fluorescence in situ hybridization (FISH) visualizes specific DNA sequences in cells, aiding in diagnostics. Comparative genomic hybridization (CGH) compares genomic content between samples to identify copy number variations. Flow cytometry enables rapid cell analysis based on their physical and chemical characteristics. Multiplex-FISH enhances FISH by allowing simultaneous detection of multiple targets, facilitating comprehensive genomic studies and improving diagnostic accuracy in clinical settings.</p></p>
