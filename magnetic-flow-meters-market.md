<p><p>Magnetic flow meters operate by utilizing electromagnetic principles to measure the flow of conductive liquids. The AC (Alternating Current) magnetic flow meters use alternating magnetic fields, providing high sensitivity and better performance in varying flow rates, often suited for large-scale applications. Conversely, DC (Direct Current) magnetic flow meters utilize a constant magnetic field, ideal for stable and continuous flow measurements. Both types are widely used in industries like water treatment, chemical processing, and food and beverage, depending on the application requirements.</p></p>
