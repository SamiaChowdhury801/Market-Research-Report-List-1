<p><ul><li>Capacitive Touch</li><li>Infrared Touch</li><li>Resistive Touch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Interactive Smart Blackboard market comprises various touch technologies: capacitive, infrared, and resistive. Capacitive touch employs conductive materials, allowing for multi-touch and precise interaction, ideal for collaborative environments. Infrared touch uses a grid of light beams to detect touch, offering durability and responsiveness, suitable for large displays. Resistive touch relies on pressure applied to layers to register input, making it cost-effective and resistant to dust. Each technology serves different educational and presentation needs, enhancing interactive learning experiences.</p></p>
