<p><p>The Omega-3 polyunsaturated fatty acid market comprises three primary types: Docosahexaenoic acid (DHA), Eicosapentaenoic acid (EPA), and Alpha-linolenic acid (ALA). DHA is crucial for brain health and is commonly found in fish oils. EPA supports cardiovascular health and is often derived from marine sources. ALA, primarily sourced from plant oils like flaxseeds, benefits heart health and is a precursor to DHA and EPA. These fatty acids are increasingly popular in dietary supplements, food products, and functional foods.</p></p>
