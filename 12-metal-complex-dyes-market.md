<p><p>The 1:2 metal complex dyes market is primarily categorized into two types: non-sulfonated and mono-sulfonated types. Non-sulfonated dyes lack sulfonic acid groups, enhancing their solubility in organic solvents, making them suitable for applications in textiles and plastics. In contrast, mono-sulfonated dyes contain one sulfonic acid group, improving water solubility and stability while maintaining vibrant color properties. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, including dyeing fabrics and producing high-performance inks, each offering unique advantages for specific applications.</p></p>
