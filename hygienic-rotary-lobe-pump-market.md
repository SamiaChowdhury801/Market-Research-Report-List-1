<p><p>The hygienic rotary lobe pump market is categorized into self-priming and conditioned self-priming types. Self-priming pumps can create a vacuum to draw in fluids without needing manual priming, making them ideal for applications with variable liquid levels. Conditioned self-priming pumps, on the other hand, are designed to operate effectively with various viscosities and solid content, ensuring reliable performance in demanding conditions. Both types are essential in industries such as food, pharmaceutical, and biotechnology, where hygienic standards are crucial.</p></p>
