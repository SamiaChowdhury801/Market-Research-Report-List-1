<p><p>Laparoscopic tumor ablation encompasses various techniques aimed at destroying tumor cells with minimal invasiveness. Microwave ablation uses electromagnetic waves to generate heat, effectively killing tumor tissues. Radiofrequency (RF) ablation employs electrical currents to create heat, targeting cancerous cells. Chemical ablation involves injecting substances that induce necrosis in tumors. Cryotherapy utilizes extreme cold to freeze and destroy abnormal tissues. Other methods may include laser ablation or high-intensity focused ultrasound, offering diverse options for tumor treatment based on individual patient needs.</p></p>
