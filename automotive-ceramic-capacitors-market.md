<p><p>The automotive ceramic capacitors market is categorized into single-layer and multiple-layer types. Single-layer capacitors offer simplicity and high reliability, making them suitable for basic applications in automotive electronics. In contrast, multiple-layer capacitors stack several layers of dielectric material, enhancing capacitance while minimizing size. This makes them ideal for advanced applications requiring compact design and higher performance. As automotive technology evolves, the demand for both types is driven by the need for improved electronic stability and efficiency in vehicles.</p></p>
