<p><p>The medium truck-mounted crane market features three main types: folding boom, telescopic boom, and truss boom. Folding boom cranes are compact and versatile, allowing for easy maneuverability in tight spaces. Telescopic boom cranes extend their arm using sections that slide out, providing great height and reach for various lifting tasks. Truss boom cranes consist of a lattice structure, offering enhanced stability and strength for heavy loads over longer distances. Each type caters to specific operational needs across various industries.</p></p>
