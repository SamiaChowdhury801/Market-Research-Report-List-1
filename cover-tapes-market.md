<p><strong>The Cover Tapes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat Activated Cover Tapes</li><li>Pressure-sensitive Cover Tapes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cover tapes market is primarily divided into two types: heat-activated cover tapes and pressure-sensitive cover tapes. Heat-activated cover tapes bond when exposed to heat, making them ideal for applications requiring strong adhesion, such as electronics and automotive industries. Conversely, pressure-sensitive cover tapes adhere upon the application of pressure, offering versatility in various packaging and protection applications, including in pharmaceuticals and consumer goods. Both types cater to distinct needs, ensuring secure coverage in diverse industrial sectors.</p></p>
