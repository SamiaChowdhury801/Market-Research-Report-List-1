<p><strong>The Display Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LED</li><li>OLED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The display panel market primarily consists of two main types: LED and OLED. LED panels utilize liquid crystal displays (LCD) backlit by light-emitting diodes, offering bright images and energy efficiency. In contrast, OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diodes) technology allows each pixel to emit its own light, resulting in deeper blacks and more vibrant colors. While LED is prevalent in budget-friendly devices, OLED is favored in high-end TVs and smartphones for its superior picture quality and thinner design.</p></p>
