<p><p>T-Shank jigsaw blades come in various types tailored for specific materials and applications. HCS (High Carbon Steel) blades are ideal for cutting softwoods and plastics, offering flexibility and durability. HSS (High Speed Steel) blades excel in cutting through metals and are heat-resistant, making them suitable for tougher tasks. Carbide blades are designed for highly abrasive materials, ensuring longevity. BIM (Bimetal) blades combine the flexibility of HCS with the durability of HSS, making them versatile for cutting diverse materials effectively.</p></p>
