<p><ul><li>In-line Vertical Static Sputtering System</li><li>In-line Vertical Dynamic Sputtering System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The In-line Vertical Sputtering System market comprises two main types: the In-line Vertical Static Sputtering System and the In-line Vertical Dynamic Sputtering System. The Static system is designed for consistent, batch processing, enabling high-quality deposition on substrates without movement, ideal for applications requiring uniformity. Conversely, the Dynamic system involves the continuous movement of substrates through the sputtering chamber, facilitating higher throughput and efficiency, making it suitable for large-scale production in industries like electronics and optics.</p></p>
