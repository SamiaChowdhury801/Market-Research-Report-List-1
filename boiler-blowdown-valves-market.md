<p><p>The boiler blowdown valves market can be segmented by blowdown type and product type. Blowdown types include intermittent and continuous systems; intermittent blowdown removes impurities at specific intervals, while continuous blowdown maintains water quality through constant discharge. In terms of product type, valves can be categorized as top or bottom blowdown valves. Top blowdown valves discharge water from the top of the boiler to reduce water levels, while bottom blowdown valves remove sludge and sediment from the boiler's bottom.</p></p>
