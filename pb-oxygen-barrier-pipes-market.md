<p><ul><li>Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H)</li><li>Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PB Oxygen Barrier Pipes market features two primary types: Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H) and Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R). PB-H is characterized by its high durability and resistance to stress cracking, making it suitable for various piping applications. In contrast, PB-R offers enhanced flexibility and impact resistance, catering to more demanding conditions. Both types serve as effective oxygen barrier solutions, preventing the ingress of oxygen in plumbing systems, thereby extending the longevity and efficiency of heating and cooling applications.</p></p>
