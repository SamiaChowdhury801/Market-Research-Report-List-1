<p><ul><li>Plug-in Charging Infrastructure</li><li>Overhead Charging Infrastructure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The E-bus charging infrastructure market comprises two primary types: plug-in charging and overhead charging. Plug-in charging infrastructure involves dedicated charging stations where buses connect via cables, allowing for convenient overnight charging or depot-based operations. In contrast, overhead charging infrastructure utilizes suspended wires or devices to deliver power directly to the bus while it is in motion, enabling rapid, on-route charging. Both systems cater to the growing demand for electric buses, enhancing their operational efficiency and supporting sustainable public transport solutions.</p></p>
