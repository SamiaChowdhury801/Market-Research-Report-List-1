<p><ul><li>Monofilament</li><li>Coiled</li><li>Coated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The endoscopy guidewire market is categorized into three main types: monofilament, coiled, and coated guidewires. Monofilament guidewires are made from a single strand, offering stiffness and pushability, ideal for precise navigation. Coiled guidewires feature a helical design, providing flexibility and torque control, useful in complex anatomies. Coated guidewires come with a specialized surface treatment to reduce friction and enhance maneuverability, allowing for smoother passage through tight spaces. Each type serves distinct procedural needs in endoscopic interventions.</p></p>
