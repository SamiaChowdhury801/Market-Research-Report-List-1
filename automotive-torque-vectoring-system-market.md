<p><strong>The Automotive Torque Vectoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive torque vectoring system market can be categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I systems involve the distribution of torque between the front and rear axles, enhancing stability and traction during acceleration and cornering. In contrast, Type II systems provide more precise control by varying torque between individual wheels on the same axle, improving handling and maneuverability. Both types aim to enhance vehicle performance, safety, and driving experience, particularly in challenging road conditions.</p></p>
