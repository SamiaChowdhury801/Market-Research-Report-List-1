<p><p>The industrial Kammprofile gasket market is segmented into three primary types. The basic Kammprofile gasket features a smooth outer surface and a serrated inner profile, providing effective sealing under high pressure. The Kammprofile gasket with an integral outer ring incorporates a rigid outer rim for enhanced stability and positioning within the flange. Lastly, the Kammprofile gasket with a loose outer ring allows flexibility during installation, accommodating flange misalignment while maintaining a secure seal. Each type caters to specific industrial sealing needs.</p></p>
