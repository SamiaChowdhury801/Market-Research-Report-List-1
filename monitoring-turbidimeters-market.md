<p><p>The turbidimeters market encompasses various types based on measurement principles. Scattered light turbidimeters use light scattered at specific angles to determine turbidity levels, while transmitted light turbidimeters measure the light that passes through a sample, detecting turbidity based on the reduction of light intensity. Transmitted scattered light turbidimeters combine both methods for enhanced accuracy. Other types may include advanced technologies or applications suited for specific industries, ensuring precise monitoring of turbidity in water quality management across various sectors.</p></p>
