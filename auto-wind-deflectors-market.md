<p><p>Auto wind deflectors, designed to reduce wind noise and deflect rain, come in two main types: In-Channel and Tape-On. In-Channel deflectors are installed directly into the window channel, offering a sleek, integrated look and durability. Tape-On deflectors are attached to the exterior using adhesive tape, providing easier installation and flexibility. Both types enhance driving comfort by improving airflow and reducing turbulence, catering to different preferences for aesthetics, installation ease, and vehicle compatibility.</p></p>
