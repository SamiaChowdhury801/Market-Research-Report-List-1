<p><p>The cermet market is categorized into three main types: carbide-based, oxide-based, and nitride-based materials. Carbide-based cermets, known for their hardness and thermal stability, are commonly used in cutting tools. Oxide-based cermets, characterized by their resistance to oxidation and high temperatures, are suitable for applications in aerospace and energy sectors. Nitride-based cermets offer excellent wear resistance and mechanical strength, making them ideal for high-performance applications in automotive and manufacturing industries, enhancing durability and efficiency.</p></p>
