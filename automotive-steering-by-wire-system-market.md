<p><ul><li>MS</li><li>HPS</li><li>EHPS</li><li>EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive steering-by-wire market comprises several types: Mechanical Steering (MS), Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), and Electric Power Steering (EPS). MS relies on traditional mechanical linkages, while HPS utilizes hydraulic pressure for assistance. EHPS combines hydraulic systems with electric motors for improved response and efficiency. EPS relies entirely on electric motors, offering enhanced control and adaptability. These systems are integral to modern vehicle technology, enhancing safety, fuel efficiency, and the driving experience.</p></p>
