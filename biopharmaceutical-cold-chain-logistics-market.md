<p><p>Biopharmaceutical cold chain logistics is critical for transporting temperature-sensitive products. Active cold chain systems utilize powered equipment like refrigeration units to maintain precise temperatures throughout the transport process. In contrast, passive cold chain systems rely on insulated packaging with temperature-regulating materials, relying on the inherent thermal properties to maintain conditions without active power. Additionally, other market types may include hybrid systems that combine elements of both active and passive methods, catering to specific product needs across the biopharmaceutical sector.</p></p>
