<p><p>The agricultural detergent market is categorized into three main types: acid, neutral, and alkaline detergents. Acid detergents are effective in removing mineral deposits and organic residues, crucial for equipment used in acid-sensitive applications. Neutral detergents are versatile, suitable for general cleaning without damaging surfaces, making them ideal for everyday use. Alkaline detergents excel at breaking down grease and fats, vital for maintaining cleanliness in equipment exposed to heavy organic matter. Each type serves specific cleaning needs in agricultural settings.</p></p>
