<p><p>The automotive integrated circuit (IC) market encompasses various types of circuits critical for vehicle electronics. Monolithic integrated circuits are single-chip solutions that offer high efficiency and compact design, suited for functions like power management and signal processing. Hybrid integrated circuits combine both discrete components and integrated circuits, providing flexibility for more complex applications, such as advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). Both types are essential in meeting the increasing demands for reliability and performance in modern automotive systems.</p></p>
