<p><strong>The Fiber-coupled Modulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SM Fiber</li><li>SMPM Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber-coupled modulators market is primarily segmented into two types: Single-Mode (SM) Fiber and Specialty Multi-Mode (SMPM) Fiber. SM Fiber is designed for long-distance communication, offering low loss and high signal integrity, making it ideal for telecommunications and high-speed data transfer. In contrast, SMPM Fiber supports multiple modes and is typically used in applications requiring high bandwidth over shorter distances, such as data centers and local area networks. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing optical signal modulation efficiency.</p></p>
