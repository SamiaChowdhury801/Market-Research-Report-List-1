<p><p>Electronic Design Automation (EDA) software encompasses several market types crucial for electronic design processes. SIP (System in Package) focuses on integrating multiple chips into a single module. CAE (Computer-Aided Engineering) aids in simulating and analyzing designs for performance and reliability. The PCB (Printed Circuit Board) market involves tools for designing circuit boards, optimizing layouts, and ensuring connectivity. MCM (Multi-Chip Module) refers to packaging multiple chips into a single housing, promoting miniaturization and improving functionality in devices.</p></p>
