<p><strong>The Synthetic Resin Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Type</li><li>Water Type</li><li>Solvent-free Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic resin coating market is categorized into three main types: solvent type, water type, and solvent-free type. Solvent type coatings use organic solvents to dissolve resins, providing durability and a smooth finish but emitting volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Water type coatings utilize water as a primary solvent, offering lower VOC emissions and safer application. Solvent-free type coatings contain minimal to no solvents, promoting environmental sustainability while delivering strong adhesion and chemical resistance. These categories cater to diverse industry needs, emphasizing performance and eco-friendliness.</p></p>
