<p><p>The Zinc Oxide Photocatalyst market comprises three primary types: Photocatalyst Solution, Photocatalyst Particle, and Photocatalyst Powder. Photocatalyst Solutions are liquid formulations that facilitate chemical reactions under light exposure, ideal for applications in water treatment and air purification. Photocatalyst Particles are solid forms that enhance surface reactions, often utilized in coatings and composites. Photocatalyst Powder, a fine powdered form, is effective for various industrial applications, providing greater surface area and catalytic efficiency, thus promoting environmental sustainability.</p></p>
