<p><ul><li>Active Sonobuoy</li><li>Passive Sonobuoy</li><li>Special Purpose Sonobuoy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sonobuoy market is categorized into three main types: active sonobuoys, passive sonobuoys, and special purpose sonobuoys. Active sonobuoys emit sound pulses and detect returning echoes to locate underwater objects, making them effective for active sonar applications. Passive sonobuoys, on the other hand, listen for sounds generated by submarines or other marine activities, enabling silent detection. Special purpose sonobuoys are designed for specific applications like environmental monitoring or advanced research, providing tailored solutions in various maritime environments.</p></p>
