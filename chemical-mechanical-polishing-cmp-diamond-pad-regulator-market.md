<p><strong>The Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) Diamond Pad Regulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plated Type</li><li>Brazed Type</li><li>Sintered Type</li><li>CVD Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) diamond pad regulator market consists of four main types: Plated, Brazed, Sintered, and CVD. Plated types feature a thin layer of diamond adhered to a substrate, offering cost-effectiveness. Brazed types involve bonding diamonds to a metal base for enhanced durability. Sintered types are created by fusing diamond particles under high pressure, resulting in a robust pad. CVD types utilize chemical vapor deposition to produce a uniform diamond layer, providing high precision and performance in CMP applications.</p></p>
