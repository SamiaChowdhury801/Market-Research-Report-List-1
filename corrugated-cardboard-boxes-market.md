<p><p>The corrugated cardboard boxes market features three main types: single, double, and triple corrugated boxes. Single corrugated boxes consist of one layer of fluted paper sandwiched between two liners, suitable for lightweight items. Double corrugated boxes have two layers of fluting, offering enhanced strength and durability for heavier or fragile products. Triple corrugated boxes, with three layers of fluting, provide maximum protection for very heavy items or those needing extra cushioning during shipping and handling. Each type serves specific packaging needs based on product weight and fragility.</p></p>
