<p><p>The energy-efficient windows market comprises primarily three types: double glazing, triple glazing, and low-emissivity (Low-E) glazing. Double glazing features two panes of glass with an insulating layer, significantly reducing heat transfer. Triple glazing incorporates three panes, enhancing thermal performance further and minimizing noise. Low-E glazing involves a microscopically thin coating that reflects heat back indoors, optimizing energy retention. Together, these products improve energy efficiency in buildings, reduce heating and cooling costs, and enhance overall comfort.</p></p>
