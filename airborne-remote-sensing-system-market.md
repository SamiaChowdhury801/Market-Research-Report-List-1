<p><strong>The Airborne Remote Sensing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Remote Sensing</li><li>Passive Remote Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airborne remote sensing system market is divided into two primary types: active and passive remote sensing. Active remote sensing systems emit their own signals, such as radar or LiDAR, to capture data about the Earth's surface, providing high-resolution images regardless of lighting conditions. In contrast, passive remote sensing systems rely on natural sunlight or thermal emissions to detect and analyze surface characteristics, typically used for applications like land cover mapping and environmental monitoring. Both methods serve distinct purposes in data collection and analysis.</p></p>
