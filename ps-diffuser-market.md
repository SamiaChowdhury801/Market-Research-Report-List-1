<p><p>The PS diffuser market offers two primary types: single frosted and double-sided frosted diffusers. Single frosted diffusers provide a soft light diffusion on one side, making them ideal for applications where space is limited and a gentle illumination is desired. In contrast, double-sided frosted diffusers deliver a uniform light diffusion from both sides, enhancing brightness and versatility, perfect for installations where lighting needs to be emitted from multiple angles. Both types are widely used in residential and commercial lighting solutions.</p></p>
