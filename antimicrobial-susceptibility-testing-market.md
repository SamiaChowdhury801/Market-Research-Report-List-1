Antifungal susceptibility testing determines the sensitivity of fungi to antifungal agents, essential in treating infections like candida. Antiparasitic susceptibility testing assesses parasites' response to antiparasitic drugs, aiding in the management of diseases such as malaria. Additionally, other susceptibility testing encompasses novel antimicrobial agents and lesser-studied pathogens, expanding treatment options.</p></p>
