<p><ul><li>Forward</li><li>Rear View</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive parking sensor market is generally categorized into two main types: forward and rear view sensors. Forward sensors are designed to detect obstacles in front of the vehicle, aiding drivers during tight maneuvers like parallel parking or navigating crowded areas. Rear view sensors, on the other hand, focus on detecting objects behind the vehicle, enhancing safety while reversing. Both types utilize ultrasonic or radar technology to provide audio or visual alerts, thereby improving overall parking safety and convenience.</p></p>
