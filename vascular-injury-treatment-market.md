<p><p>The vascular injury treatment market encompasses various surgical techniques aimed at repairing blood vessels. Lateral arteriorrhaphy/venorrhaphy involves suturing torn arteries or veins. Patch angioplasty uses a patch to widen narrowed vessels. Bypass grafting creates a new pathway for blood flow around obstructions. Extraanatomic bypass reroutes blood flow outside of the affected area. Thrombectomy involves removing blood clots from vessels. Other treatments may include endovascular techniques or adjunctive therapies, addressing diverse vascular damage needs and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
