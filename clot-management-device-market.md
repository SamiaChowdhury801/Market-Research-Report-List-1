<p><p>The clot management device market is segmented into several types, including Inferior Vena Cava Filters (IVCF) that prevent venous thromboembolism, Neurovascular Embolectomy Devices designed to remove clots from cerebral vessels, and Catheter-Directed Thrombolysis (CDT) Devices that directly dissolve blood clots. Additionally, Percutaneous Thrombectomy Devices mechanically remove clots from blood vessels, while Embolectomy Balloon Catheters facilitate clot removal by inflating a balloon to dislodge obstructions. Each device caters to specific clinical needs in managing thrombosis-related conditions.</p></p>
