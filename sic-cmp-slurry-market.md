<p><ul><li>Bulk Removal Slurry</li><li>Fine Polish Slurry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SiC CMP slurry market consists of two main types: bulk removal slurry and fine polish slurry. Bulk removal slurry is designed for the efficient removal of larger amounts of material during initial processing stages, making it suitable for heavy stock removal on SiC substrates. In contrast, fine polish slurry is utilized for achieving smooth, defect-free surfaces in the final stages of polishing, enhancing the quality and performance of SiC wafers used in semiconductor applications.</p></p>
