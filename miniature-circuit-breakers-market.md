<p><p>The Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCBs) market is categorized into three types: B Type, C Type, and D Type MCBs. B Type MCBs are designed for residential applications, protecting against overloads and short circuits in lighting circuits. C Type MCBs are suitable for commercial and industrial use, managing moderate inrush currents, commonly found in motors and transformers. D Type MCBs handle high inrush currents, making them ideal for heavy machinery and equipment. Each type caters to specific electrical load requirements and provides necessary protection.</p></p>
