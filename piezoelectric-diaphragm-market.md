<p><p>A piezoelectric diaphragm is a thin, flexible component that converts electrical energy into mechanical vibrations or vice versa, utilizing the piezoelectric effect. It is commonly used in applications such as speakers, buzzers, sensors, and microphones due to its ability to generate sound or detect pressure changes efficiently.
