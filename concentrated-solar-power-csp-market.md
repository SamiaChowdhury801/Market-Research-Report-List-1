<p><p>Concentrated Solar Power (CSP) utilizes mirrors or lenses to focus sunlight onto a small area, generating heat to produce electricity. Market types include Trench CSP systems, which use linear parabolic concentrators to track the sun; Tower-type systems, where mirrors direct light to a central tower, heating a fluid; and Dish CSP systems, which utilize parabolic dishes to concentrate sunlight onto a receiver. Other market options include hybrid systems and integration with thermal energy storage for enhanced efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
