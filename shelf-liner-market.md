<p><ul><li>Meshed</li><li>Smooth</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shelf liner market primarily consists of two types: meshed and smooth liners. Meshed shelf liners are designed with a textured surface, allowing for better air circulation and moisture drainage, making them ideal for kitchen environments. Smooth liners, on the other hand, provide a sleek, flat surface that's easy to clean and offers a more refined aesthetic, suitable for various applications like living rooms or bathrooms. Both types enhance organization and protect surfaces from scratches and spills.</p></p>
