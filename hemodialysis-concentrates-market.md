<p><ul><li>Acid Concentrates</li><li>Bicarbonate Concentrates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hemodialysis concentrates market comprises acid and bicarbonate concentrates, essential components in the dialysis process. Acid concentrates, which contain electrolytes and acids, are mixed with purified water to form dialysate, assisting in the removal of waste products from the blood. Bicarbonate concentrates, on the other hand, provide alkalinity and help maintain the body's pH balance during treatment. Both types are crucial for effective dialysis sessions, catering to the needs of patients suffering from renal failure.</p></p>
