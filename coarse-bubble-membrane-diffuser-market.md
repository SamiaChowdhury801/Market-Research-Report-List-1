<p><p>The coarse bubble membrane diffuser market consists of two primary types: disc diffusers and tube diffusers. Disc diffusers feature a flat, circular design, facilitating efficient air distribution and high oxygen transfer rates, making them ideal for large tanks. Tube diffusers, on the other hand, are elongated and typically installed in rows, offering flexible configurations and effective aeration in various water treatment applications. Both types enhance wastewater treatment efficiency and are crucial in maintaining aquatic ecosystems and supporting biological processes.</p></p>
