<p><p>Liquid epoxy resin is a versatile thermosetting polymer widely used in coatings, adhesives, composites, and electronics due to its excellent mechanical properties, chemical resistance, and strong adhesion. Its applications span industries such as construction, automotive, aerospace, and marine, where high-performance materials are essential.</p><p>The Liquid Epoxy Resin Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.2% during the forecast period.
