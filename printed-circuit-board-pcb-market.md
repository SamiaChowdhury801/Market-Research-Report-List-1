<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) market is categorized into three main types: single-sided, double-sided, and multilayer panels. Single-sided PCBs feature components on one side, making them cost-effective and suitable for simple applications. Double-sided PCBs have circuitry on both sides, allowing for more complex designs and compact layouts. Multilayer PCBs contain multiple layers of circuitry, enabling high-density connections and advanced functionalities, essential for intricate electronic devices. Each type caters to varying complexities and requirements in the electronics industry.</p></p>
