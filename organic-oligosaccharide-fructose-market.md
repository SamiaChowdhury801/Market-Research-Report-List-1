<p><p>The Organic Oligosaccharide Fructose (FOS) market is categorized into two main types: Liquid FOS and Crystal FOS. Liquid FOS is a syrupy form that offers high solubility and is commonly used in beverages and food products, enhancing sweetness without a significant calorie increase. Crystal FOS, on the other hand, presents as a powdered form, ideal for baking and as a sugar substitute in dry applications. Both types provide prebiotic benefits, supporting digestive health and promoting gut flora.</p></p>
