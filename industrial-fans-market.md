<p><strong>The Industrial Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Fans</li><li>Axial Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial fans market primarily comprises two types: centrifugal fans and axial fans. Centrifugal fans utilize a rotating impeller to move air, generating high-pressure airflow ideal for ventilation, cooling, and material handling applications. In contrast, axial fans move air parallel to the axis of the fan, producing lower pressure but higher airflow, making them suitable for general ventilation and cooling tasks. Both fan types are crucial in various industries, including manufacturing, HVAC, and agriculture, ensuring efficient air movement.</p></p>
