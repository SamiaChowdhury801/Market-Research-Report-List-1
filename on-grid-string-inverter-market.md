<p><p>The on-grid string inverter market is categorized into two main types: single-phase and three-phase string inverters. Single-phase string inverters are typically used for residential applications, where lower power demands make them ideal for smaller solar installations. In contrast, three-phase string inverters are suited for commercial and industrial applications, handling higher power outputs and allowing for greater scalability. Both types convert the direct current generated by solar panels into alternating current for use in the grid, enhancing energy efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
