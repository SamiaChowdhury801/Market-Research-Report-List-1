<p><p>School attendance software is available in two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, allowing easy access from various devices, facilitating real-time updates, and reducing IT maintenance costs. Conversely, on-premises software is installed locally on school servers, offering greater control over data security and customization but requiring more significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Both types serve to streamline attendance tracking and enhance administrative efficiency in educational institutions.</p></p>
