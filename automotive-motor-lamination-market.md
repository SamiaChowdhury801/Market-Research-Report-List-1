<p><strong>The Automotive Motor Lamination Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Rolled Non-Oriented (CRNO)</li><li>CRGO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Motor Lamination Market includes Cold Rolled Non-Oriented (CRNO) and Cold Rolled Grain-Oriented (CRGO) laminations. CRNO laminations are widely used in electric motors due to their uniform magnetic properties in all directions, making them ideal for applications like EV motors and alternators. CRGO laminations, with superior magnetic properties in a specific direction, are primarily used in transformers and high-efficiency motors. Both types reduce energy loss, enhance performance, and are critical in automotive electrification and energy-efficient systems.</p></p>
