<p><strong>The Nanoimprint Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Embossing (HE)</li><li>UV-based Nanoimprint Lithography (UV-NIL)</li><li>Micro Contact Printing (µ-CP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nanoimprint equipment market features several key types: Hot Embossing (HE), which uses heat and pressure to transfer patterns onto substrates; UV-based Nanoimprint Lithography (UV-NIL), which employs ultraviolet light to cure polymer films and form nanoscale patterns; and Micro Contact Printing (µ-CP), a technique that utilizes an elastomeric stamp to print nanoscale features onto surfaces. Each method offers unique advantages for high-resolution patterning in applications such as electronics, optics, and biomedicine, driving innovation in nanofabrication.</p></p>
