<p><p>The Hydrogen Fuel Cell Forklift market includes PEMFC (Proton Exchange Membrane Fuel Cell) forklifts, DMFC (Direct Methanol Fuel Cell) forklifts, and others. PEMFC forklifts use hydrogen gas and are known for high efficiency, quick refueling, and zero emissions, making them ideal for indoor use. DMFC forklifts utilize methanol as fuel, offering easier storage and handling but with lower efficiency. Other types may include solid oxide or alkaline fuel cells, catering to niche applications with varying performance and operational requirements.</p></p>
