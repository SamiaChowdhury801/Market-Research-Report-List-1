<p><p>The Cross-Laminated Timber (CLT) market is primarily segmented into two types: adhesive-bonded CLT and mechanically fastened CLT. Adhesive-bonded CLT utilizes strong adhesives to bond layers of wood in a crosswise manner, providing enhanced durability and structural integrity. Conversely, mechanically fastened CLT employs hardware like bolts or screws to assemble the layers, offering easier disassembly and potential environmental benefits. Both types cater to various construction needs, promoting sustainable building practices and efficient use of wood resources.</p></p>
