<p><p>The fiber-optic gyroscope market is categorized into single-axis and multi-axis types. Single-axis gyroscopes measure rotation around a single axis, making them suitable for applications requiring basic navigation, such as in automotive systems. In contrast, multi-axis gyroscopes can measure rotation across multiple axes simultaneously, providing comprehensive motion sensing for complex applications like aerospace, robotics, and marine technologies. Both types leverage the principles of fiber optics to deliver high precision and reliability in motion detection and orientation.</p></p>
