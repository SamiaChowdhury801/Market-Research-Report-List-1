<p><ul><li>Single Piston Caliper</li><li>Multi-Piston Caliper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motorcycle brake caliper market includes two main types: single-piston and multi-piston calipers. Single-piston calipers use one piston to apply braking force, offering simplicity, lighter weight, and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for entry-level or lightweight motorcycles. Multi-piston calipers feature multiple pistons, providing better braking performance, heat dissipation, and modulation, ideal for high-performance or heavy bikes. While single-piston calipers are more affordable and easier to maintain, multi-piston calipers deliver superior stopping power and precision, catering to riders prioritizing safety and performance.</p></p>
